The Rotating Token Concept

From what I understand, AI-powered bots can now solve CAPTCHAs with high accuracy. Traditional security measures are becoming obsolete as AI evolves exponentially.


I'm new to formal cybersecurity. This is my way of sharing what I've learned. Take it, use it, make it better.

  • Dynamic Defense: Tokens change faster than AI can adapt
  • Resource Drain: Forces attackers to constantly re-authenticate
  • Time Advantage: Buys crucial seconds for intrusion detection
  • Quantum Resistance: Even quantum computers shouldn't be able to predict truly random tokens

While AI can defeat static defenses, rotating everything possible creates moving targets. This idea is not about stopping AI completely, it's about slowing attacks, detering some, making them more expensive and detectable.
